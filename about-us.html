Welcome to Medicare, a group of professionals from primary and secondary health care, who are passionate in delivering quality health care services for the public. Medicare is definitely a roof build up by Specialists, General Practitioners, Nurses, Patient Advocates, Therapists, Pathologists, Pharmacists and other reputed members from the health care industries, who are dutiful and motivated to serve for the health and well-being of the people and the society. We firmly believe that by developing into an easily accessible Healthcare service, it enables us to steadily contribute to grow a healthy society ahead.
