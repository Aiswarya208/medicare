New Born and Mother Care

Having a life coming from you is nothing but a miracle. But the journey of a mother is not easy. The hormones, the mood swings, the anxiety is nothing short of overwhelming. But it sky rockets once the little one has emerged. And that’s why a mother and especially a new mother would need all the support she can during this magical period. We, at Medicare, recognizes this and has come forward with our New Born and Mother Care packages where a mother could get all the support she can have to take care of not just her baby, but also herself.

  • Assessment of growth and development, including monitoring of vitals of both baby and mother
  • Umbilical cord care
  • Eyes, ears, nose care
  • Wound care for mother
  • Circumcision care
  • Feeding and lactation consultation
  • Bathing, Feeding, Hygiene assistance
  • Assistance in developing sleep routine for baby
  • Scheduling and assisting in the immunization of baby
  • Involving and supporting the mother for physical movement and activity
  • Educating the mother on holding techniques, feeding techniques, diet, etc
  • Follow up medical assistance.

Why Choose Us

  • Premium, Professional care to you and your baby
  • Our Nurses are well experienced and extensively trained
  • Experts in handling new born baby
  • Choose between a Licensed Nurse or a Certified Caregiver
  • Choice for flexible packages – any time of the day, any number of hours
  • Tailored packages which work with your schedule
